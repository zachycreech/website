about

Hey! I am Zach Wheeler, aka Zachy. I am a lover of simple things like coffee, the color purple, video games, and spicy things. I have always had a passion for computers. In school I would push a ice cream cart on the beach to afford broken computers and parts to fix. I started repairing computers in Geeksquad after high school professionally and became even more hooked. After selling a computer to a district manager of Mosaic in the Microsoft advertising, I was offered a job on the team. This got me interested in software development, web development, and UX/UI development. I started to binge on coding simple games, websites, and installing every linux distro I could fit on my hard drive.

I joined the Navy served my country and now I am here. Ready to learn more, develop skills, meet people smarter than me, and have a great time. If you want to learn more about me or my projects, send me an email and lets get in touch!
