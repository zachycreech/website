about

Hey! I'm Zach Wheeler, aka Zachy. I'm a lover of simple things like coffee, the color purple, video games, and spicy things. I have always had a passion for computers. In school, I would push an ice cream cart on the beach to afford broken computers and parts to fix. I started repairing computers at Geeksquad after high school professionally and became even more hooked. After working my way up, I began working for Microsoft as a Brand Ambassador. This got me interested in software development, web development, and UX/UI development. I started to binge on coding simple games, websites, and installing every linux distro I could fit on my hard drive.

I joined the Navy, served my country, and now I am here. Ready to learn more, develop skills, meet people smarter than I am, and have a great time. If you want to learn more about me or my projects, send me an email and lets get in touch!
